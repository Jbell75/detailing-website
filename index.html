About Us

At ShinePro Detailing, we are passionate about cars and dedicated to delivering meticulous detailing services that restore and protect your vehicle’s beauty. Located in Nashville, Tennessee, we combine years of experience with top-tier products to ensure every car leaves our shop looking better than new.

Our team specializes in exterior washes, paint correction, interior deep cleaning, and advanced ceramic coatings. We believe in transparency, quality, and customer satisfaction. From daily drivers to luxury sports cars, your vehicle is in expert hands.
